Nasal Covid Vaccine has been developed by Bharat Biotech. Vaccine has successfully completed Phase 1 clinical trial in age groups of 18 to 60 years. According to the officials, doses of this vaccine administered to healthy volunteers in Phase I clinical trial were well-tolerated. It was found to be safe, immunogenic and well tolerated during the pre-clinical toxicity studies. It was able to evoke high level of neutralizing antibodies in studies on animals. The nasal vaccine is the first of its kind COVID-19 vaccine that is undergoing human clinical trials in India.
